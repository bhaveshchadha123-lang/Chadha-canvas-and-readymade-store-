About Chadha Canvas

Chadha Canvas is a leading supplier of premium agricultural protection materials serving farmers across the region. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing high-quality tarpaulin sheets and green shade nets at affordable prices.

Our Story

We started Chadha Canvas with a simple mission: to provide farmers with affordable, high-quality agricultural protection products. Understanding the challenges farmers face with harsh weather, monsoons, and intense sunlight, we sourced the best materials and competitive pricing to make quality protection accessible to everyone.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with farmers across the region. Our products have successfully protected countless acres of crops and storage facilities, earning us the trust and loyalty of our customers.

Today, Chadha Canvas continues to grow by maintaining our core values of quality, affordability, and customer service. We are proud to be a trusted partner in agricultural protection.

Our Mission
