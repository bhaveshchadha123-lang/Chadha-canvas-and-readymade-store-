Frequently Asked Questions

How long do your products last?

Our tarpaulin sheets last 3-5 years depending on weather conditions. Green shade nets typically last 2-3 seasons with proper care.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We provide special pricing for bulk orders. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

What is the delivery timeframe?

Standard delivery within 3-5 business days. We also offer express shipping for urgent orders.

Which shade percentage should I choose?

For most vegetables: 40-60%. For delicate plants and nurseries: 60-80%. Contact us for personalized recommendations.

Do you provide custom sizes?

Yes! We can provide customized sizes for large orders. Please contact us with your specifications.

Do you offer warranty?

All our products come with quality guarantee. We handle defects and damages within 30 days of purchase.
